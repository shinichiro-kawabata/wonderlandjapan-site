FAQ

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, especially on our private tours. For the Osaka Soul Food tour, we can accommodate vegetarian requests with advance notice. Please let us know when booking.

Are the tours suitable for children?

Our walking tours are family-friendly. The food tours involve walking and bar-style seating, so we recommend them for children ages 12 and up.

What happens if it rains?

We run rain or shine! Kyoto looks beautiful in the rain. In cases of dangerous weather (typhoons), we will cancel and offer a full refund.
